The Week Forward

Vicky Sivakumar



Is it going to give you the list and the details of movies that is going to be released this week?

Well yes, but it does a little bit more. We’re going to give you the urge to watch movies based on the cast and crew’s history. This way, on a very miniature scale, the low budgeted “good”movies wouldn’t be missing out on their fortunes and you, ‘the audience’ wouldn’t be griping that your 150 bucks went to shit. Perceive the seventh sense the right way.

THUPPARIVALAN

Language: Tamil
Director: Mysskin
Cast: Vishal, Prasanna, Vinay, Bhagyaraj, Andrea

Thupparivaalan (aka) Thuparivalan is a Tamil action, spy, thriller film written and directed by Mysskin. The film is produced by Vishal, who also stars in the lead role. Vinay, Prasanna, Rakul Preet Singh and K. Bhagyaraj also play prominent roles.

Vishal plays a private detective named Kaniyan Poongundran in the movie Thupparivaalan and Prasanna, as Mano, assists Vishal by aiding in his cases. Prasanna’s character would be along the lines of Dr. Watson from Arthur Conan Doyle’s Sherlock Holmes novels.



Leela (Simran) and her family are celebrating her husband’s birthday in their terrace. After giving surprise gifts to her husband, it is shown that her husband and son are dead stroked by lightning. But Leela feels it as unnatural death, so she comes to Kaniyan to investigate her husband and son’s death. Did Kaniyan accept the case and did he find the reason for their death? The rest of the story is to be watched on screen.

MAGALIR MATTUM

Language: Tamil
Director: Bramma
Cast : Jyothika, Bhanupriya, Saranya, Urvashi

Magalir Mattum (Magalir Mattum 2017) is a Tamil drama movie written and directed by Kuttram Kadithal fame Bramma. The film features Jyothika in the lead role while Saranya, Bhanupriya, Urvashi, Nassar and Livingston play pivotal roles.

Magalir Mattum produced by Suriya, the venture began production in July 2016. The satellite rights of the film were sold to Jaya TV.

YAAR IVAN

Language : Tamil
Director: Satya Tatineni
Cast : Sachlin Joshi, Esha Gupta, Delhi Ganesh, Sathish

Veedevadu in Telugu, Yaar Ivan in Tamil is an upcoming Indian bilingual action drama film written and directed by Tatineni Satya. Shot in Telugu and Tamil, the film features Sachiin J. Joshi and Esha Gupta in the lead roles, with Prabhu also in a pivotal role. Featuring music composed by S. Thaman, the venture began production in May 2015.

KALATHUR GRAMAM

Language: Tamil
Director: Saran K Adwaithan
Cast: Kishore, Yagna Shetty, Tarun Shatriya

Kalathur Gramam is a period film of genre ‘Action-Drama’ covering 3 decades of the 70s-80s-90s. In a single line, the story narrates an era of dispute between a father and son. The plot portrays the life happenings in a village ‘Kalathur Gramam’ which is located near Pattukkottai, Thanjavur district of Tamil Nadu.

The movie is being shot in Tuticorin District Ettayapuram taluka T.Puthupatti. We chose a village there after looking at over 125 villages all over Tamil Nadu . A secluded village with about 10 families living there, where a set was built exclusively by Mr.Thotta Tharani and team. Costumes are a key factor in this movie. We have made sure that it is a subtle but a stand-out factor in the movie.

Costumes have been designed specially to depict the period differences. Praveen has keen eye to detailing and that ensures the differentiation from period to period.

The movie being action oriented, special focus has been given to keep every fight as realistic as possible. The stunts are interesting yet believable. There are no special effects or filmy larger than life fights.

Music Direction is by Maestro Ilayaraja, and this marks his 1005th film.

KOMBAY

Language: Tamil
Director: Hafiz Ismail
Cast: Charles Arun, Theertha, Prithvi Rajan

Set in a rural area around Theni, in the village called Kombay, the movie tells the story of Vetri, a ruthless gangster who will kill anyone for money. Problems arise when the delinquent Vetri falls in love with a girl named Kodi, a middle-class girl, who does not approve of his work and wants him to change, which results in Vetri having more rivals.

AMERICAN ASSASSIN

Language: English
Director: Michael Cuesta
Cast: Dylan O’Brien, Michael Keaton

When Cold War veteran Stan Hurley takes CIA black ops recruit Mitch Rapp under his wing, they receive an assignment to investigate a wave of random attacks on both military and civilian targets. After discovering a pattern of violence, Hurley and Rapp join forces with a lethal Turkish agent to stop a mysterious operative who wants to start a global war.

LUCKNOW CENTRAL

Language: Hindi
Director: Ranjit Tiwari
Cast: Farhan Akhtar, Diana Penty, Rajesh Sharma

Kishen Mohan Girhotra (Farhan Akhtar) is a young man who belongs to Moradabad, a city in Uttar Pradesh. One fateful day, Kishen gets accused of murder which lands him in Lucknow Central Jail where he’s awaiting his High Court trial for a death penalty. Gayatri Kashyap (Diana Penty), a diligent NGO worker, is compelled to form a “Band of Prisoners” for the band competition which is to be held in the dreaded jail of ‘Lucknow Central’ that year and this is how Kishen’s and her paths cross. Kishen befriends four other inmates, namely Dikkat Ansari, Victor Chattopahdyay, Purushottam Pandit, Parminder Trehan and convinces them to join the band. The dramatic narrative of Lucknow Central portrays how Kishen’s life progresses in jail and how music becomes an intricate part of not only his journey, but the rest of the band’s as well.

SIMRAN

Language: Hindi
Director: Hansal Mehta
Cast: Kangana Ranaut, Mark Anthony Justice

A Gujarati housekeeping lady in the US allows ambition to get the better of her & gets involved in a world of crime. Simran is a racy, fun film with Kangana Ranaut playing the titular role.

UNGARALA RAMBABU

Language: Telugu
Director: Kranthi Madhav
Cast: Prakash Raj, Miya George, Sunil

Ungarala Rambabu movie is a romantic comedy entertainer written and directed by Kranthi Madhav and produced by Paruchuri Kiriti under United Kriti Movies banner while Ghibran scored music for this movie.

Sunil and Miya George are playing the main lead roles along with Prakash Raj, Posani Krishna Murali, Ali and Vennela Kishore are seen in crucial roles.

KATHALO RAJAKUMARI

Language: Telugu
Director: Mahesh Surapaneni
Cast: Nara Rohit, Naga Shourya, Namitha Pramod

Kathalo Rajakumari movie is a romantic action entertainer directed by debutante Mahesh Surapaneni and produced by Venkat Srinivas Boggaram under Magnus Cine Prime banner while Ilayaraja has scored music for this movie.

Nara Rohit and Namitha Pramod are playing the main lead roles in the movie.

PATEL KI PUNJAABI SHAADI

Language: Hindi
Director: Sanjay Chhel
Cast: Rishi Kapoor, Paresh Rawal, Payal Ghosh

The story is about a Punjabi, played by Rishi Kapoor and a Gujarati, played by Paresh Rawal, whose children are getting married to each other. Payal Ghosh will debut as Paresh Rawal’s daughter, who plays the love interest of Rishi Kapoor’s son, to be played by Vir Das.

SARAASUDU

Language: Telugu
Director: Pandiraj
Cast: Silambarasan, Nayanthara, Andrea

Simbu plays an IT professional, who takes us into his past to unravel his first love and the heartbreak he goes through thereafter.

He begins his life afresh with Nayanthara and hopes for a better luck when it comes to his love life, second time around. But, how difficult is it to move on in one’s life?

Being the dubeed version of ‘Idhu Namma Aalu’, it is about a married couple and their relationship which needs a bit of sparkle. Without much twists and turns, Pandiraj presents a neatly woven light-hearted entertainer.

OUR PREDICTION

This week, Magalir Mattum and Thupparivalan are expected to go neck to neck in the box office with Mysskin and Bramma going head to head. Both directors are expected to deliver on their potential with subjects as intricate as mystery and feminism. Lucknow Central and Simran also have some hype surrounding them with their star-studded cast. But, this week’s winners are surely ‘Thupparivalan’ and ‘Magalir Mattum’.
