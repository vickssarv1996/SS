The Week Forward

Surya



Is it going to give you the list and the details of movies that is going to be released this week?

Well yes, but it does a little bit more. We’re going to give you the urge to watch movies based on the cast and crew’s history. This way, on a very miniature scale, the low budgeted “good”movies wouldn’t be missing out on their fortunes and you, ‘the audience’ wouldn’t be griping that your 150 bucks went to shit. Perceive the seventh sense the right way.

THE DARK TOWER

Language: English
Director : Nikolai Arcel
Cast : Idris Elba, Matthew McConaughey

A kid who has prescience, foresees a gunslinger in an alternate world. He eventually meets the guy and they both embark on a journey in an attempt to protect the “Dark Tower” that protects both the worlds from evil.

This fantasy sci-fi movie is an official adaptation of the famous Stephen king’s Dark tower series. The series consists of about 8 books and we don’t know if the movie is a compressed version of the books or if there’d be sequels. King’s sci-fi novels have worked on and off with the audience. Everything mentioned above gets sidelined by the cast; King has assembled for the movie. Idris Elba and McCanoughey play the good and the bad guys respectively. The film production estimates should be very high as the visual grandeur portrayed looks effervescently lively. It’s been long since a proper sci-fi had released and we really hope it lets us escape reality and transcend into the world they project.

BABUMOSHAI BANDOOKBAAZ

Language: Hindi
Director : Gaurav Dagaonkar
Cast : Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Bidita Bag

The film is set in a North Indian village where Siddiqui plays a gangster. There’s some heavy stereotyping around Bollywood that a gangster movie should have blatant lust depictions and gruesome killings and gore. This venture does the same but if the movie gives a proper reason to use those, then there’s no stopping the laurels it would receive.
This controversial movie surfaces itself to the theaters finally after the negative aura that engulfed it since the beginning. The team decided to release a song video from the movie and the CBFC decided to make 48 cuts in that one single video. It became more popular this way than it would’ve ethically.
Negatives aside, Nawazuddin Siddiqui leads this movie and it could and would be a great reason to watch it in the big screen.
Siddiqui – “The Dark Horse“ is very much known for his script selection and his subtle yet dark intimidating acting skills. Anurag Kashyap should be lauded for finding the amazing talent the man has amassed through whatever effort he had put in. For those who have doubts, please do watch “Gangs of Wasseypur“.

QAIDI BAND

Language : Hindi
Director : Habib Faisal
Cast : Aadar Jain, Anya Singh, Sachin Pilgaonkar

Qaidi Band is a musical drama. 7 under-trail prisoners combine to make a musical video and the video gets viral on social media. The aftermath is what the movie deals with.
It’s an honest and noble attempt is what one feels after watching the trailer. The film’s flourished with rustic and pastoral portrayal.
Not much is known about the cast. The lead girl debuts in this movie.
Is it worth a watch? We’ll have to wait.

ARJUN REDDY

Language : Telugu
Director : Sandeep Reddy Vanga
Music : Raadhan
Cast : Vijay Deverakonda, Shalini Pandey

The protagonist’s course of life is what the trailer projects.The hero becomes a drug addict because of the most obvious reason in Indian cinema, “ love failure”. Clichéd much?
But with some clever editing and an angry tint flowing through the music and emotions, the editor has subsumed the audience effectively.
The film has gained much buzz before its release as the promotion of the movie was blazing like a rocket. The fan base of Vijay Devarakonda have many moments in the theater to scream their heart out as the slow-mo scenes and the heavy metal BGM go hand in hand.

OUR PREDICTION

With Vivegam’s release, nothing can come close in terms of collections at the box office. One might say that the release of small budget movies juxtaposing a huge movie is nonsensical.
But what is life without risk eh?
However among the films releasing today, Babumoshai Bandookbaaz should be winning as it has gained a lot of reception through both negative and positive publicity. Trust in Nawazuddin Siddiqui as his history’s been free from betrayals.
