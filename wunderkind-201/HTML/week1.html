The Week Forward

Surya



Is it going to give you the list and the details of movies that is going to be released this week?

Well yes, but it does a little bit more. We’re going to give you the urge to watch movies based on the cast and crew’s history. This way, on a very miniature scale, the low budgeted “good”movies wouldn’t be missing out on their fortunes and you, ‘the audience’ wouldn’t be griping that your 150 bucks went to shit. Perceive the seventh sense the right way.

VIVEGAM

Language : Tamil
Director : Siva
Music director : Anirudh Ravichander
Cast : Ajith Kumar, Kajal Agarwal, Akshara Hasan

Ajith Kumar’s “Vivegam” marks his 25th year in the cine industry.

Amidst much opposition and criticism, the actor has gone on to complete his third movie under director Siva. The female lead is played by Kajal Agarwal and Akshara Hasan makes her debut in a very “important role” as quoted by the director. The movie has its music done by Anirudh Ravichander and editing by Ruben whose notable works were “Raja rani” and “Remo”. The movie as we understand is said to be a spy thriller. Vivegam has to go big in the box office for it to compete with its proper rival for we all know what it is. While Ajith Kumar’s fans have gone amuck in booking tickets and declaring it a blockbuster, we would just have to wait to see how the movie rolls out.

VETTAI KARUPPAR AYYA

Language : Tamil
Director : Amigoz Sugo
Cast : Raam King, Gpkay
Vettai Karuppar Ayya is a movie with an entire new cast and crew. The film is directed by ‘Amigo Sugu’. Yeah, that is what the credits showed.
The trailer was a sequence of scenes with a lady wearing some black makeup walks across and suddenly the camera zooms to her face where she shows her sparkling teeth. I infer from this and only this that the movie is of horror genre. We see a swamiji raging and using a ‘Kamehameha‘ power bomb on the supposed ghost.
A very iconoclastic attempt on cinema from the director. Eager to see the feedback the movie’s going to earn after its release.

ORU VISHESHAPETTA BIRIYANI KISSA

Language : Malayalam
Director : Kiran Narayanan
Music director : Bijibal
Cast : Aju Varghese , Vinay Forrt
This malayalam movie competes with the biggie mentioned before.Trailer doesn’t reveal much but we could outright guess the movie’s genre to be a fantasy comedy. Odds stacked up against this movie is too damn high, but remember, a turtle does win at times.

OUR PREDICTION

This needs no rocket science as this week’s winner at box office would for sure be Vivegam. It is estimated to earn a gross of 100 crore.
But the real winning moment would be for Ajith to act in a sensible movie once again and prove his critics wrong, as his script selection for the past 5 – 6 years has been very weird and surprising. Really hope director Siva delivers something digestible.
